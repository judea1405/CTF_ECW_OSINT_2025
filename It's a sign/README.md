## Challenge Prompt
We are on the trace of an enemy agent. He appears to have escaped to a country that does not seem to be France.
A photo has been leaked, locate it and give us its approximate location.

---

## Flag Format
Submit the **Plus Code** (global code) of the location derived from the photo: `ECW{8CWW4889+29}`
- The example above shows the **required structure**: 8 characters + 4 characters (with a **+** before the last two).  
- Your answer must be a **valid Plus Code** for the approximate spot visible in the image.
