## Challenge Prompt
“Who put a tree here?!”

Your friend recorded a crash of his FPV drone into a **tree** and even left a **motor** on the ground. Using only open-source information and the provided **profile picture**, find the **exact coordinates of the tree** where the crash happened.

---

## Flag Format
`ECW{.*, .}`
- Submit **latitude, longitude** in **decimal degrees**, separated by a comma and a space.
- **Round to 3 decimal places** (standard rounding).
- Include the minus sign if applicable (W/S hemispheres).

---

## Provided Files
`profile_picture.* (image)`

## Submission
- Example (format only):  `ECW{48.857, 2.295}`
- Ensure you’ve rounded both latitude and longitude to **three decimals** and kept the comma + space separator.
